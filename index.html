Other important notes about Revival Nights-

Photos/videos/livestreaming will not be allowed during the “Private Event portion” of the evening out of respect the privacy of others and will be strictly enforced. Those not following this reasonable request will be politely asked by our security team to leave the property immediately and will be escorted out.

The Private Event Group Prayer Opportunity will be announced/offered to ALL guests before dismissal time with more details and information at that time.

Please bring your own lawn chairs as chairs/seating will be very limited during this season of growth.

We encourage you to bring your own hydration drinks, water, etc... NO alcoholic beverages, smoking, vaping, chewing tobacco allowed on property out of respect for the other guests attending.

Public restrooms will be available onsite near our event.

Our team cannot wait to meet you!

Water Baptism

Baptism is something to celebrate! Water Baptism is the public expression of your decision to follow Jesus. If you’ve never been water baptized or would like to recommit your life to God and be re-baptized, just mention this to our team at the check-in table when you arrive at the Revival Nights event so we can be prepared for you.

Guests who come to the Revival Night with interested in water baptism are encouraged to bring an extra set of clothes and a towel. Please plan to dress modestly. We ask that no excessively short (mid-thigh) cut off shorts, dresses or swimsuits to be worn in the baptism tank... Darker shirts, shorts and pants/jeans are okay.

Towels are very limited at this time so please bring your own.

Public restrooms will be available nearby for changing into dry clothes.

Matthew 3:11 KJV- I indeed baptize you with water unto repentance. but he that cometh after me is mightier than I, whose shoes I am not worthy to bear: he shall baptize you with the Holy Ghost, and with fire:
